Caravan

Caravancabs provides you to search nearby cabs by fetching your source address and lets you know the estimated cost as per your destination.

Once you got nearby cabs, you can compare them on the basis of various factors viz. ETA, price, cab-category, cab-providers and then can enjoy your ride with your favourite option.

Caravan redirects you to book your favourite cab to the cab-provider's app. Also it lets you call the cab. We'll soon be coming with On App booking.
